Project Overview

NearBeat helps people discover spontaneous nearby real-world events.

Users can:

Discover nearby events

↓

View event details

↓

Join the event

↓

Chat with attendees

↓

Meet in person

NearBeat intentionally avoids social media mechanics.

There are:

No followers
No likes
No comments outside event chats
No direct messaging
No communities
No payments
No advertisements

Everything revolves around nearby real-world events.
